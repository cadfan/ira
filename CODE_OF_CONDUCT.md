# Code of Conduct

This is a personal project shared with the iRacing community. While contributions are welcome, please keep interactions respectful and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in discussions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on the issue or code, not the person
- Accept that the project owner has final say on direction and inclusion of changes

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Spam or off-topic content
- Sharing others' private information

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may result in comments being removed or users being blocked from the repository.

## Questions

Open an issue for project-related questions. For conduct concerns, contact the repository owner directly.
